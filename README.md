A professional Sales & Revenue Analysis Dashboard developed using Power BI to analyze business performance through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking. The dashboard provides detailed insights into total sales, revenue trends, profit analysis, top-performing products, category-wise performance, and regional sales distribution.

The project includes data importing from Excel, data transformation, KPI card creation, interactive charts, slicers, and business insight generation to help users understand sales patterns and make data-driven decisions. Various visualizations such as line charts, bar charts, pie charts, and tables are used to present data in a clear and professional manner.

Key Features:
• Sales and Revenue KPI Tracking
• Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis
• Top Performing Products Analysis
• Region-wise and Category-wise Sales Insights
• Interactive Filters and Slicers
• Professional Dashboard Design
• Business Insight Generation
• Excel Dataset Integration

Technologies Used:
Power BI, Excel, Data Visualization Techniques, KPI Analytics, and Business Intelligence Concepts.
